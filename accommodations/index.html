Travel & Housing

 

Getting to Colorado

By Road

If you're driving from Texas, plan for a 15-18 hours on the road.  We'd recommend stopping overnight at higher altitude to help you acclimate.

By Air

The closest airports to Eureka are in Durango (DRO) and Montrose (MTJ) .  Because of some road construction scheduled for next summer, we'd recommend flying into Durango.  The number of flights in and out of the region is limited, so please book early! 

Rental cars are available at the Durango Airport.  There are also several airport transit options including a complimentary shuttle service for guests staying at the Hilton DoubleTree and service from Buckhorn Limousine, Animas Transportation, and Durango Taxi.

Getting to Eureka Lodge

Eureka Lodge is located outside of Silverton, but you won't need to find it yourself.  There's not much parking at the lodge (and the road to get there is a one lane mountain dirt road) so we're providing shuttle service from Durango and Silverton before and after the wedding events on Saturday.  We'll publish schedules and pick-up locations soon!

 

Accommodations

We've blocked rooms in Silverton and Durango, the two towns closest to  Eureka Lodge.  If you'd like to look for a cabin, house, or other kind of housing, there are lots of options on Airbnb and VRBO, but book soon, since summer is high season in this area.


Durango

Durango is a fun town with lots of options for eating, shopping, and entertainment.  However, it is about an hour and a half drive from the wedding venue, so please plan accordingly!

Strater Hotel

The Strater Hotel is located on the main street of historic downtown Durango within easy walking distance of restaurants, shopping, and the famous narrow gauge railroad terminal.

We have single and double rooms blocked until June 8th.  Call 800-247-4431 and ask for the Hause O'Connor Wedding to book.

Hilton DoubleTree

Situated on the Animus river near the historic downtown district of Durango, the Hilton Doubletree hotel offers more modern accommodations and amenities a bit further from the bustle of Main Avenue.

We have additional rooms blocked until June 8th.  Please follow the link here to reserve one.

Other Options

Durango offers a wide variety of hotels, B&Bs, and other accommodations such as the General Palmer Hotel and a Best Western Inn.  

Silverton

Silverton is a smaller former mining town that now caters to mountain adventure sports and enthusiasts.  Situated at 9,300 feet elevation, it's not for those prone to altitude sensitivity, but it is located close to the Eureka Lodge.  Everything in Silverton is walkable, as it is a very small town.  Housing in Silverton is limited, so please book early if you'd like to stay here.

Grand Imperial Hotel

Similar to the Strater Hotel in Durango, the Grand Imperial is a restored Victorian building with lots of historical appeal.  

We have a number of rooms blocked.  Call 970-387-5529 and mention that you're with the Hause O'Connor wedding to reserve yours.  However, rooms will be released to the general pool after April 9, so please book early if you'd like to stay here.

Other Options

There are several additional small Victorian hotels and B&Bs in Silverton, including the Teller House and Wyman Hotel
